# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Never share OTPs or personal information with anyone
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails or messages
- Always check the website URL before entering sensitive data
- Enable two-factor authentication for security
- Keep your devices and software updated regularly
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Verify payment requests before making UPI transactions
- Regularly check bank statements for unauthorized activity
- Report cybercrime incidents on https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 for immediate assistance
